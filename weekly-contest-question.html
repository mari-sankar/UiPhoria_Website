In various professional and academic settings, there is a frequent need to modify documents for readability and presentation purposes. A common task involves extracting text from PDF documents, adjusting the font and font size, and then converting the modified document back to PDF. This process can be time-consuming and prone to errors when done manually. Develop an automated bot that can read text from a PDF document, convert it to a Word document, change the font and font size as specified by the user, and then re-convert the modified document back to PDF.

Your task is to create an RPA automation that :

1. Reads text in input PDF. 2. Writes in word document.

3. Change font to “Times New Roman” and font size to 12

Problem Statement:

Henry Dev, a diligent professional, struggles with the tedious and error-prone task of manually creating and distributing certificates to numerous recipients. Each certificate requires meticulous attention to detail, and the process of verifying and sending them to the correct individuals is time-consuming and frustrating. Despite his best efforts, mistakes in delivery occur, leading to dissatisfaction and additional workload. Recognizing the inefficiency, Dev decides to develop a bot that automates the entire process, from certificate creation to accurate distribution. This bot aims to save time, reduce errors, and streamline the workflow, significantly enhancing efficiency and accuracy.

Your task is to create an RPA automation that :

1. Develop a bot to automate the editing of names and dates in the PowerPoint certificates.

2. Implement a system for accurately matching and sending certificates to the correct email IDs.

3. Ensure the bot converts the edited PowerPoint certificates to PDFs before sending.

4. Avoid using any websites for the PPT to PDF conversion; instead, handle the conversion efficiently during the saving process.

5. Streamline the workflow to save time and reduce errors.

6. Enhance overall efficiency and accuracy in certificate distribution.
