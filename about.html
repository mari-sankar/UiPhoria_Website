About Us

UiPhoria is a club Initiative at Rajalakshmi Engineering college as an academic alliance of UiPath based On Robotics Process Automation. We're your go-to squad for simplifying the tough stuff and ensuring everyone wins. Our vibe? Think collaborative projects, workshops, and a chill space for cooking bots. We're all about RPA innovation fused with data precision.
